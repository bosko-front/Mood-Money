# MoodMoney Support

Effective Date: 2025-12-04

Thank you for using MoodMoney. This document explains how to get help, what to expect from us, and what information to include so we can resolve issues quickly.

## Contact Options
- Primary email: support@moodmoney.app
- Alternate email: boskojaksic2407995@gmail.com
- In-app: Settings → Help / Support (where available)

Please use a single channel per request to avoid duplicates. If you email us from an address different from your app store account email, mention the app store email in your message for account lookups.

## Support Hours and Response Targets
- Hours: Monday–Friday, business hours (excluding regional holidays)
- First response target: within 1–2 business days
- Urgent service impact (see Severity P0/P1 below): best-effort within 12 business hours

We strive to resolve most issues in the first reply. Complex issues may require additional time for investigation.

## Severity Levels and Handling
- P0 – Critical outage/security: App cannot be used at all, widespread crash, data leak suspected.
  - Action: Email with subject starting "P0:". We prioritize immediately and may request logs or disable affected features temporarily.
- P1 – Major impact: Key feature unusable, repeated crashes on common devices.
  - Action: Email with subject starting "P1:". We aim to provide a workaround or fix in the next update.
- P2 – Normal: Non-blocking bug, performance issue, UI glitch.
- P3 – Low/Question: How-to questions, minor requests, general feedback.

## What to Include
Please provide as much of the following as possible:
- Description: What you expected vs. what happened
- Steps to reproduce: Step-by-step list
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible; redact sensitive data)
- Device: Model (e.g., iPhone 14), OS version
- App version: Visible in app Settings (or from the App Store/Play Store listing)
- Network: Wi-Fi/Cellular, VPN/Proxy if any
- Time of occurrence and your timezone
- Account identifier: The email used with the app store and/or app login (if applicable)

## Billing and Subscriptions
Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play using their in-app purchase systems (with RevenueCat infrastructure). As a result:
- Manage or cancel subscriptions:
  - iOS: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions
  - Android: Google Play → Menu → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
- Receipts and refunds: Governed by Apple/Google policies. Please request refunds via the respective store.
- If something looks wrong with your entitlement inside the app after a successful purchase, contact us with your app store receipt/email and we’ll help investigate.

## Data Rights Requests
You can request a copy of your data or ask for deletion:
- Email: support@moodmoney.app
- Subject: "Data Request" or "Data Deletion Request"
- Include: App store email, any in-app user ID (if known), and a brief description.
We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling the request. See PRIVACY_POLICY.md for more details.

## Security
If you believe you’ve found a security or privacy vulnerability:
- Email: support@moodmoney.app
- Subject: "Security Report"
- Do not disclose publicly until we’ve confirmed and addressed the issue.
We appreciate responsible disclosure and will acknowledge valid reports.

## Feature Requests and Feedback
We welcome ideas to improve MoodMoney. When suggesting a feature, please include:
- The problem you’re trying to solve
- Why it matters to you
- Any examples of similar solutions you like
- Priority/impact (nice-to-have vs. must-have)

## Troubleshooting Basics
Try these steps before contacting support (often resolves common issues):
- Ensure you’re on the latest app version
- Force-quit and reopen the app
- Log out and log back in (if applicable)
- Toggle network connection (Wi-Fi ↔ Cellular)
- Reboot your device
- If a purchase didn’t unlock features, use the "Restore Purchases" option in Settings (if available)
